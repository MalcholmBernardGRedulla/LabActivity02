Table Tennis

  • Invented in England: Table tennis was invented in the late 19th century in England as a parlor game. It was originally called "Whiff-Whaff".
  • Fast-paced Game: Table tennis is known for its incredibly fast pace, with professional players able to hit the ball at speeds exceeding 60 mph. The game requires quick reflexes and precise timing.
