Badminton

  • Fastest Racket Sport: Badminton is the fastest racket sport in the world. The shuttlecock can travel at speeds of over 200 mph, making it a game of quick reflexes and strategic play.
  • Origin: Badminton originated from a game called "Poona" played in India during the 19th century. British army officers stationed there brought the game back to England, where it evolved into modern badminton.
  • Olympic Sport: Badminton was introduced as an official Olympic sport in 1992 in Barcelona. It quickly gained popularity, especially in Asian countries, where it is a dominant sport.
