Volleyball

  • Invented for Accessibility: Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan as a less physically demanding alternative to basketball, making it accessible to a broader range of participants, including older players.
  • Global Popularity: Volleyball is one of the most popular sports worldwide, with an estimated 800 million players across the globe, making it a staple in recreational and professional sports.
  • Olympic Sport: Volleyball became an official Olympic sport in 1964, and the beach volleyball variant was added to the Olympics in 1996, both attracting large global audiences.
